## Plan details

Pantheon offers a single subscription plan:

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Monthly price | $50.00 |
| Billing cycle | Monthly |
| Payment processor | Stripe |
| Cancellation | Anytime, effective end of billing period |

## What you get

Your subscription activates your Pantheon workspace, which includes:

- **Built-in farm tools** — Scale tickets, grain bids, weather forecasts, memory
- **Three built-in skills** — Grain bids, weather forecasts, scale tickets
- **Multi-agent support** — Up to 4 specialized agents
- **Extension marketplace** — Access to additional capabilities
- **Dashboard** — Usage analytics, settings management
- **Automated scheduling** — Cron jobs for morning briefings and daily bid updates

## Activating your subscription

During onboarding, you'll be redirected to Stripe's secure checkout:

1. Click "Subscribe" on the activation prompt
2. Enter your payment information on the Stripe checkout page
3. Complete the purchase
4. You're redirected back to Pantheon to continue onboarding

<Callout variant="info" title="Secure payments">
All payment processing is handled by Stripe. Pantheon never sees or stores your credit card number. Stripe is PCI Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security.
</Callout>

## Managing your subscription

Access your subscription settings at **Settings > Billing**:

### Update payment method

Click "Manage Billing" to open the Stripe customer portal where you can update your card on file, switch payment methods, or add a backup payment method.

### View invoices

The Stripe portal shows your complete billing history with downloadable PDF invoices for each billing period.

### Cancel your subscription

You can cancel anytime from the Stripe portal. Your workspace will remain active until the end of your current billing period. After that:

- Your AI assistant goes offline
- Your data (farm profile, scale tickets, configuration) is retained for 30 days
- You can reactivate within 30 days to restore everything

<Callout variant="warning" title="Data retention">
After 30 days of cancellation, your workspace data including scale ticket records may be permanently deleted. Export any important data before canceling.
</Callout>
