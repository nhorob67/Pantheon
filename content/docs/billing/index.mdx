## How billing works

Pantheon uses a straightforward pricing model with two components:

1. **Monthly base subscription** — A fixed $50/month fee that covers your AI assistant and platform access
2. **Metered API usage** — Billed at cost based on the actual AI model tokens your assistant consumes

This structure keeps the base price low while ensuring you only pay for what you use.

## What's included in the base subscription

Your $50/month subscription includes:

- Your AI assistant, available 24/7
- All three built-in skills (grain bids, weather, scale tickets)
- Multi-agent support with personality presets
- Built-in farm tools (scale tickets, grain bids, weather, memory)
- Access to the extension marketplace
- Dashboard with usage analytics
- Encrypted configuration storage

## What counts as API usage

Every time your AI assistant processes a message — whether it's answering a grain bid question, generating a weather briefing, or reading a scale ticket photo — it uses tokens from the underlying AI model (Claude by Anthropic).

Token usage varies by task:

- Simple question/answer: ~500–1,000 tokens
- Grain bid fetch with comparison: ~2,000–4,000 tokens
- Scale ticket photo OCR: ~3,000–5,000 tokens
- Morning weather briefing: ~1,500–3,000 tokens

For a typical farm operation, metered usage runs **$5–15/month** depending on how actively you use the assistant.

## Managing your billing

All billing is managed through **Settings > Billing** in your Pantheon dashboard. From there you can:

- View your current plan and billing period
- See your metered usage breakdown
- Update your payment method
- Access invoices and billing history
- Cancel your subscription

Payments are processed securely through Stripe. Pantheon never stores your credit card information directly.
