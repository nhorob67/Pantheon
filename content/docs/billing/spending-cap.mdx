# Spending Cap

The spending cap lets you set a maximum monthly budget for metered AI usage. Once your assistant reaches the cap, it pauses automatically -- protecting you from unexpected charges.

## Setting a spending cap

1. Go to **Settings > Billing** (or **Settings > Spending Cap**)
2. Enter your monthly spending limit ($1--$1,000)
3. Toggle **Auto-pause at cap** to control whether your assistant stops when the limit is reached
4. Optionally set an **alert email** to receive spending notifications
5. Click **Save**

The cap applies to metered API usage only -- it does not affect your $50/month base subscription.

## Spending alerts

Pantheon sends alerts at three thresholds:

| Threshold | Severity | Notification |
|---|---|---|
| **50%** of cap | Info | Dashboard notification |
| **80%** of cap | Warning | Dashboard + email alert |
| **100%** of cap | Critical | Dashboard + email alert |

Pantheon also detects **spending anomalies** -- if today's usage is more than 3x your daily average (and over $0.50), you'll receive a warning.

Spending is checked automatically every 4 hours.

## What happens when the cap is reached

When auto-pause is enabled and your metered usage reaches the spending cap:

- Your assistant **stops responding** to new messages in Discord
- Scheduled jobs (morning weather, daily grain bids) are **paused**
- Your dashboard shows a clear indicator that the cap is active

<Callout variant="warning" title="Assistant pauses at the cap">
When the spending cap is reached with auto-pause enabled, your assistant will not respond until the next billing period starts or you increase the cap. Make sure your cap is set high enough for your normal usage.
</Callout>

## Resuming after a pause

Your assistant resumes automatically when you:

- **Increase the cap** above your current month's spending -- the pause clears immediately
- **Remove the cap** -- set the value to $0 or clear the field
- **Wait for the next billing period** -- usage resets at the start of each month

## Recommended cap levels

| Usage Level | Recommended Cap |
|---|---|
| Light (a few questions/day) | $15--20/month |
| Moderate (regular daily use) | $25--30/month |
| Heavy (frequent use, multiple agents, OCR) | $40--50/month |

If you're unsure, start with a $30 cap and adjust based on your actual usage.

## Monitoring usage

Track your spending in real time from the dashboard:

- **Current period usage** -- How much you've spent so far this billing period
- **Daily breakdown** -- Token usage and cost per day
- **Cap utilization** -- Progress bar showing how close you are to your cap

See [Metered Usage](/docs/billing/metered-usage) for details on what drives costs and how to reduce usage.
