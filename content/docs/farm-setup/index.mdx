# Farm Setup

Before your FarmClaw AI assistant can start helping you, it needs to know the basics about your operation. Farm setup is the first step in onboarding and takes about five minutes. The information you provide here drives everything your assistant does -- from pulling the right grain bids to calculating bushel weights on scale tickets.

Farm setup covers three key areas:

## Farm Profile

Your farm profile includes your farm name, state, county, crop selection, and total acreage. This is the foundation that personalizes your entire FarmClaw experience. Your state selection determines your timezone, the elevator presets available to you, and the weather data sources used for forecasts.

[Set up your farm profile](/docs/farm-setup/farm-profile)

## Crop Selection

FarmClaw supports 10 row crops common to the Upper Midwest. The crops you select determine which commodities your assistant tracks for grain bids, which bushel weight conversions it uses for scale tickets, and how your agents are configured.

[Choose your crops](/docs/farm-setup/crop-selection)

## Elevator Configuration

Connect the grain elevators you deliver to so your assistant can pull real-time cash bids. FarmClaw includes one-click presets for major elevator companies operating in your state, and you can add any custom elevator with a public bid page.

[Configure your elevators](/docs/farm-setup/elevator-config)

---

## How It All Fits Together

Once you complete farm setup, FarmClaw uses your profile to:

- **Personalize your AI agents.** Your agents know your crops, your elevators, and your location. When you ask "what are corn bids today?", they know exactly where to look.
- **Configure grain bid tracking.** The grain bids skill scrapes your configured elevator websites daily and compares cash bids across locations.
- **Set up scale ticket management.** When you log a delivery, your assistant uses the correct bushel weights for your crops and suggests your configured elevators by name.
- **Deliver relevant weather.** Forecasts, spray windows, and growing degree day calculations are based on your farm's coordinates and timezone.

<Callout variant="tip" title="You can change these settings later">
Everything in farm setup can be updated at any time from **Settings > Farm Profile** in your dashboard. Changes are applied to your assistant automatically.
</Callout>

## What Comes Next

After completing farm setup, the onboarding wizard continues with:

1. **Location & Weather** -- Set your weather coordinates for forecasts and field conditions.
2. **Discord Connection** -- Connect your Discord server so your assistant has a place to live.
3. **Review & Launch** -- Confirm your configuration and set up your FarmClaw workspace.
