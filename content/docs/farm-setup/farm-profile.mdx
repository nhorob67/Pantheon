# Farm Profile

Your farm profile is the first thing you fill out during onboarding. It tells FarmClaw where you are, what you grow, and the size of your operation. Every other feature -- grain bids, weather, scale tickets, agent behavior -- builds on this information.

## Required Fields

All fields in the farm profile are required. Here is what each one does and what the system expects.

### Farm Name

Your farm or operation name. This is used throughout the dashboard and in your AI assistant's configuration so it can refer to your operation by name.

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Type | Text |
| Min length | 1 character |
| Max length | 100 characters |
| Example | `Johnson Family Farm` |

### State

FarmClaw currently serves six Upper Midwest states. Your state selection controls which elevator presets appear, which county list is shown, and which timezone is applied.

| Supported States |
|---|
| North Dakota (ND) |
| South Dakota (SD) |
| Minnesota (MN) |
| Montana (MT) |
| Iowa (IA) |
| Nebraska (NE) |

<Callout variant="info" title="State determines your timezone">
Your timezone is set automatically based on your state. Montana uses **America/Denver** (Mountain Time). All other supported states use **America/Chicago** (Central Time). This timezone is used for scheduling cron jobs like morning weather briefings and daily grain bid checks.
</Callout>

### County

After selecting a state, you will see a list of counties for that state. Select the county where your primary operation is located. Your county helps the system narrow down location-specific data like weather stations and regional market conditions.

### Primary Crops

Select at least one crop that you grow. Tap or click crops to toggle them on or off. Your crop selections affect:

- **Grain bids** -- Only bids for your selected crops are tracked from each elevator.
- **Scale tickets** -- Your assistant suggests your crops during ticket entry and uses the correct bushel weights for conversions.
- **Agent recommendations** -- Agent personality presets are tuned based on the commodities you produce.

See [Crop Selection](/docs/farm-setup/crop-selection) for the full list of supported crops and their bushel weights.

### Total Acres

Enter the total number of acres in your operation. This must be a whole number (no decimals) and must be a positive value up to 100,000 acres.

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Type | Integer |
| Minimum | 1 |
| Maximum | 100,000 |
| Example | `2500` |

Your total acreage is used by AI agents for contextual advice. For example, when discussing application rates, seed needs, or harvest logistics, your assistant knows the scale of your operation.

## Timezone Reference

Your timezone is populated automatically and cannot be edited manually. It is derived from your state selection:

| State | Timezone |
|---|---|
| North Dakota (ND) | America/Chicago (Central) |
| South Dakota (SD) | America/Chicago (Central) |
| Minnesota (MN) | America/Chicago (Central) |
| Montana (MT) | America/Denver (Mountain) |
| Iowa (IA) | America/Chicago (Central) |
| Nebraska (NE) | America/Chicago (Central) |

## Editing Your Farm Profile

You can update your farm profile at any time after onboarding.

1. Open your FarmClaw dashboard.
2. Go to **Settings > Farm Profile**.
3. Make your changes and click **Save Changes**.

<Callout variant="info" title="Changes take effect immediately">
When you save changes to your farm profile, FarmClaw applies the updates to your assistant. Changes take effect on the next message.
</Callout>

## Validation Summary

| Field | Type | Required | Constraints |
|---|---|---|---|
| Farm Name | String | Yes | 1-100 characters |
| State | Enum | Yes | ND, SD, MN, MT, IA, NE |
| County | String | Yes | Must match selected state |
| Primary Crops | Array | Yes | At least 1 crop selected |
| Total Acres | Integer | Yes | Positive, max 100,000 |
