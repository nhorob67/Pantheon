# Email Integration

FarmClaw's email integration lets your AI assistant send and receive email. Set up an email inbox, and your assistant can process inbound emails with attachments and compose replies -- all accessible from Discord.

## Setting up your email inbox

Email is optional and only enabled by explicit action:

1. Go to **Settings > Email**
2. Click **Enable Email Inbox**
3. FarmClaw generates a unique email address (e.g., `smith-farms@farmclaw.com`)
4. Your inbox is ready

The default slug is based on your farm name. You can customize it to any available slug (3--63 characters, lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens).

## How inbound email works

When someone sends an email to your FarmClaw address:

1. The email is received and processed (including attachments)
2. Your assistant sends an immediate acknowledgment reply ("Got your file. Analyzing now...")
3. The content and attachments are analyzed by your assistant
4. A full response is sent back via email with proper threading

Common attachments like PDFs, images, Word documents, and text files are automatically read and summarized.

## Sending email from Discord

Ask your assistant to send emails directly:

```
Email the grain contract summary to john@example.com
```

```
Send today's scale ticket report to bookkeeper@farm.com
```

Your assistant composes the email based on your request and sends it from your FarmClaw email identity.

## Customizing your email address

To change your email slug:

1. Go to **Settings > Email**
2. Edit the slug field
3. Save

The slug must be unique across all FarmClaw customers. Changes take effect immediately.

## Privacy and security

- Email processing happens within your workspace -- no data is shared between customers
- Inbound emails and attachments are stored with Row-Level Security
- Webhook signatures are verified for all inbound email events
- Idempotency keys prevent duplicate processing
