## What is Pantheon?

Pantheon gives your team a dedicated AI agent system that lives in your Discord server. Ask your agents to research topics, draft content, pull data, automate tasks, and more -- all in plain English, right from your phone or desktop.

Each Pantheon workspace is a fully configured multi-agent system powered by Claude, Anthropic's AI model. You get real-time access to the capabilities that matter to your team.

## Who is it for?

Pantheon is designed for **teams and organizations** that want AI-powered automation delivered through Discord:

- **Small businesses** looking for an always-available AI assistant
- **Operations teams** that need data retrieval and reporting
- **Remote teams** that already collaborate on Discord
- **Agencies and consultants** managing multiple workflows
- **Anyone** who wants a customizable multi-agent AI system without the infrastructure headaches

Whether you're a small team of 3 or a growing organization, Pantheon scales to your needs.

## What can it do?

Pantheon provides a flexible platform for building and deploying AI agents:

- **Custom Skills** -- Create specialized skills tailored to your team's workflows. Write them in Markdown or generate them with AI assistance.
- **Knowledge Files** -- Upload documents (PDFs, Word docs, text files) that your agents can reference when answering questions.
- **Memory Vault** -- Persistent memory across conversations so your agents remember important facts, preferences, and decisions.
- **Integrations** -- Connect to Google Sheets, Gmail, Calendar, Slack, and 800+ third-party services through Composio.

Beyond the built-in tools, the **extension marketplace** lets you add more capabilities as they become available.

## How does it work?

1. **Sign up** and activate your $50/month subscription
2. **Complete onboarding** -- name your team, configure your first agent, and connect your Discord server
3. **Your workspace is created** with your team configuration
4. **Start chatting** in Discord -- ask questions, run tasks, and interact with your agents

Your agents are always available. Scheduled messages and automated briefings can be configured to run on any cadence.
