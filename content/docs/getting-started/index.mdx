## What is FarmClaw?

FarmClaw gives your farm a dedicated AI assistant that lives in your Discord server. Ask it for cash grain bids, weather forecasts, spray window assessments, and more — all in plain English, right from your phone or desktop.

Each FarmClaw workspace is a fully configured multi-agent system powered by Claude, Anthropic's AI model. You get real-time access to the information that matters during the growing season and harvest.

## Who is it for?

FarmClaw is designed for **row crop farmers** in the Upper Midwest:

- **North Dakota**
- **South Dakota**
- **Minnesota**
- **Montana**
- **Iowa**
- **Nebraska**

Whether you're farming 500 acres of corn and soybeans or 10,000 acres of spring wheat and durum, FarmClaw scales to your operation.

## What can it do?

FarmClaw comes with three core skills out of the box:

- **Grain Bids** — Scrapes your configured elevators for current cash bids, basis levels, and delivery periods. Compare prices across elevators instantly.
- **Weather Forecasts** — Pulls forecasts from the National Weather Service. Includes spray window analysis, growing degree day calculations, and automated morning briefings.
- **Scale Tickets** — Log grain deliveries via photo OCR, voice dictation, or guided entry. Track totals by crop, elevator, and date. Export to spreadsheets.

Beyond the built-in skills, the **extension marketplace** lets you add more capabilities as they become available.

## How does it work?

1. **Sign up** and activate your $50/month subscription
2. **Complete onboarding** — set up your farm profile, elevators, weather location, and Discord server
3. **Your workspace is created** with your farm configuration
4. **Start chatting** in Discord — ask questions, get bids, log tickets, check weather

Your assistant is always available. Morning weather briefings and daily grain bid updates can be scheduled automatically.
