## Overview

This guide walks you through the entire setup process. By the end, you'll have a working AI agent in your Discord server ready to help your team.

## Step 1: Create your account

Go to the Pantheon signup page and enter your email address. We use **magic link authentication** -- no password required. Check your inbox for the login link and click it to access your dashboard.

<Callout variant="tip" title="Check your spam folder">
Magic link emails occasionally land in spam or promotions folders. The email comes from Pantheon and contains a one-time login link.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Activate your subscription

After logging in, you'll be prompted to activate your subscription:

- **$50/month** base subscription
- **Metered API usage** billed at cost (typically $5--15/month depending on usage)
- Billing is handled securely through Stripe

Click "Subscribe" to complete checkout. You'll be redirected back to Pantheon to begin onboarding.

## Step 3: Complete the onboarding wizard

The onboarding wizard has three steps:

### Team

- **Team name** -- A display name for your workspace
- **Description** -- A brief description of what your team does

### Agent

- **Configure your first agent** -- Set up your initial AI agent with a role, goal, and backstory that define its identity and behavior

### Discord

- **Add Pantheon to Discord** -- Click the invite link to add the Pantheon bot to your server, or paste your Discord Server ID directly. See the [Discord Setup](/docs/discord/server-setup) guide for details.

## Step 4: Send your first message

Once onboarding is complete, your agent is ready. Open your Discord server and try one of these:

```
What can you help me with?
```

```
Summarize the key points from [topic]
```

```
Help me draft a plan for [task]
```

Your AI agent will respond in the channel. That's it -- you're up and running.
