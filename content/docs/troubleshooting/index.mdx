## Getting help

If you run into issues with Pantheon, this section covers the most common problems and their solutions. For issues not covered here, contact support through your dashboard.

## Quick diagnostics

Before diving into specific issues, check these basics:

1. **Workspace status** — Check your dashboard. Your workspace should show "Active" status.
2. **Discord bot** — Verify the bot appears online (green dot) in your Discord server's member list.
3. **Subscription** — Confirm your subscription is active in Settings > Billing.

If all three check out and you're still having issues, read on for specific scenarios.
